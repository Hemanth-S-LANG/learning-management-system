# How to Access Teacher Notes as a Student

## Step-by-Step Guide:

### 1. Login as a Student
- Go to the login page
- Enter your student credentials
- Click "Login"

### 2. On the Dashboard
You should see **THREE buttons** for students:
1. **"View Courses"** (Blue button) - Browse and enroll in courses
2. **"My Timetable"** (Green button) - View your class schedule
3. **"My Notes"** (Yellow button) - Access your notes and teacher notes ⭐

### 3. Click "My Notes" Button
- This is the yellow button on your dashboard
- It will take you to the notes page

### 4. Select a Course
- You'll see a list of courses you're enrolled in
- Click on any course to view notes for that course

### 5. View Teacher Notes
Once you select a course, you'll see two sections:
- **"My Notes"** - Your personal notes
- **"Notes from Your Assigned Teachers"** - Notes uploaded by teachers assigned to you

## Important Notes:

### To See Teacher Notes, You Need:
1. ✅ Be enrolled in a course
2. ✅ Have a teacher assigned to you for that course
3. ✅ The teacher must have uploaded notes

### If You Don't See Teacher Notes:
- Make sure you're enrolled in the course (click "View Courses" → "Enroll")
- Check if a teacher has been assigned to you (click "My Timetable")
- The teacher needs to upload notes (they use "Upload Notes" button)

## Troubleshooting:

### Button Not Showing?
1. Make sure you're logged in as a **student** (not teacher)
2. Refresh the page
3. Clear browser cache and reload

### No Teacher Notes Showing?
1. Enroll in a course first
2. Wait for a teacher to assign themselves to you
3. Teacher needs to upload notes for that course

## Quick Test:
1. Login as student
2. Click "View Courses"
3. Enroll in a course (e.g., CS101)
4. Login as teacher (different account)
5. Click "Manage Courses"
6. Select the same course
7. Click "Assign to Me" for the student
8. Click "Upload Notes" on teacher dashboard
9. Upload a note for that course
10. Login back as student
11. Click "My Notes"
12. Select the course
13. You should see the teacher's note!
